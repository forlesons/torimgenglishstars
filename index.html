Give me salt, eggs, flour, milk, sugar, salt, butter and vegetable oil, as well as a frying pan and I'll make you pancakes. I will mix eggs, salt, sugar and stir. Add the flour and pour in the milk. I will beat the pancake batter to achieve a homogeneous consistency. I'll add vegetable oil to the dough and start frying.
